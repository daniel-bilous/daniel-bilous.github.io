Rocket Recovery System

Role: Rocket Recovery Engineer at Rice Eclipse

Timeline: August 2025 - Present

Project Overview

Led the design and development of a comprehensive rocket recovery system for Rice Eclipse's L1-Class rocket. The project involved optimizing rocket designs through advanced simulation software, structural CAD modeling, and precise engineering calculations to achieve a projected apogee of 2,960 feet.

Key Contributions

Design Optimization: Utilized OpenRocket simulation software to iterate on rocket designs, achieving a 125% increase in average projected apogee compared to previous iterations through systematic testing and refinement.

Structural Engineering: Designed critical bulkheads and interstage connections in SolidWorks, ensuring structural integrity under high-stress flight conditions while maintaining precise mechanical integration between rocket stages.

Recovery System: Calculated exact explosive charge amounts using MATLAB to guarantee safe stage separation and reliable parachute deployment, balancing safety requirements with performance optimization.

Manufacturing: Constructed the complete L1-Class rocket airframe and recovery system utilizing small-scale manufacturing processes, translating digital designs into a functional flight-ready vehicle.

Technical Skills Applied

SolidWorks CAD modeling, OpenRocket flight simulation, MATLAB computational analysis, trajectory optimization, structural mechanics, manufacturing processes, and systems integration.

Outcomes

Successfully achieved a projected apogee of 2,960 feet through comprehensive simulation and design validation. The project demonstrated proficiency in aerospace engineering principles and the ability to integrate multiple technical disciplines into a cohesive, high-performance system.

VEX Robotics Program

Role: VEX Robotics Captain at Glenbrook North High School

Timeline: January 2024 - May 2025

Project Overview

Founded and led Glenbrook North High School's first competitive robotics program from the ground up. Built a team of 10+ members and established all operational processes, technical workflows, and competitive standards to create a sustainable program for future students.

Key Contributions

Program Development: Pioneered the school's inaugural robotics program, recruiting team members, securing resources, and establishing organizational frameworks that enabled the team to compete effectively.

Operations Management: Established comprehensive operational processes including budget management, material sourcing protocols, and developed CAD training programs to bring new members up to speed on design tools.

Team Leadership: Coordinated cross-functional workflows between four specialized sub-teams (design, fabrication, programming, and strategy) under tight competition schedules, ensuring effective communication and project completion.

Performance Improvement: Implemented structured validation processes and fostered a competitive environment that drove a 40% improvement in robot performance metrics through iterative testing and team collaboration.

Technical Skills Applied

CAD design and training, budget management, project coordination, team leadership, fabrication techniques, competitive strategy development, and process optimization.

Outcomes

Successfully established a sustainable robotics program that achieved competitive performance while developing leadership, technical, and project management skills. Created training materials and operational frameworks that continue to support the program beyond my tenure as captain.
